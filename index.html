EduSpark is an online platform designed to support education and learning. It offers tools for teachers, students, and schools to improve teaching, track progress, and access educational resources. Teachers can use EduSpark for professional development, creating lessons, and monitoring student performance. Students can explore learning materials, complete courses, and earn certificates. Some versions also provide school management tools like attendance tracking, exam management, and communication with parents. EduSpark aims to make education more accessible, organized, and effective through technology.
